ABOUT

It is a non-profit website specially designed for the customer as well as the seller. Emphasis has been for both the customer and the seller that every seller can register their buziness establishment at free of cost to sell their construction materials easily . The customer who are going to build their dream house can see and compare their construction materials by sitting at the comfort of their home.

  1. How to Sell-


    For selling you have to first fill up the google form and provide your original details .Then our team will reach out to you by the provided information then your listing will be done --This process may take a little bit time.


  2. How to order-


    You can order the item by directly calling the manager of the specific store through the phone number provided by the specific store


  3. How to cancel your order-


    The ordering process is similar for cancelling the product by caling the manager.
